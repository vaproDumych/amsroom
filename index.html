YOUR BEST B&B


Located 200 m from Regardz Zilveren Toren Amsterdam and 400 m from Beurs van Berlage, Amsroom offers accommodation in a central area of Amsterdam. The Basilica of St. Nicholas and the Royal Palace Amsterdam are within 500 m and a 7-minute walk of the bed and breakfast, and free WiFi is provided.


The bed and breakfast also offers a shared bathroom.

Dam Square is a 7-minute walk from the bed and breakfast. The nearest airport is Schiphol Airport, 12 km from Amsroom.
